This chart is intended to compare popular and critically acclaimed animated films. The most well known animation studio from Japan is "Studio Ghibli". Many of their films have worldwide influence.

Something to note is that not all of the films from this list are for children. While Studio Ghibli produces family friendly content, other studios may have different intended audiences. Please keep that in mind and look up more information on a movie before showing it to children.
